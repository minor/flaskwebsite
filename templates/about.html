About Me

Hi, I'm Saurish!

I'm a sophomore at Evergreen Valley Highschool. I love biology, photography, and playing video games!

I'm the co-founder of Ataraxia, a nonprofit with a goal to improve the mental health of the homeless populations in San Jose. Ataraxia creates care kits with mental health education and materials. At Ataraxia, we strive to create a society where mental healthillnesses are more accepted and homeless populations can receive the help they need without feeling outcasted.

I'm also an avid debater. I participate on the National Circuit in Lincoln Douglas. As a freshman, I cleared at a TOC-qualifying bid tournament and read a variety of different arguments. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to me on Messenger!

Relevant coursework: AP Calculus AB, AP Physics 1

Clubs: secretary of Synthetic Biology, secretary of Volleyball, LD admin board in Speech and Debate, member of Biomedical Engineering, member of Programming.
